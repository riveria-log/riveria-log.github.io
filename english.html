Impact

Literacy has been a key factor in terms of my employability growth. It has helped broadened my worldview by exploring diverse perspectives, sharpening my critical thinking skills. It has helped my ability to convey information more clear and concisely, whether through writing or speaking, which has been crucial within projects and collaborations, both in and outside of school. It has enhanced abilities like adaptability, effective communication, and my capacity to quickly grasp new information.

Strengths

My strengths in Literacy include my ability to communicate clearly and effectively through writing. Ive always been good at organizing my thoughts and presenting ideas in a way that is easy for others to understand. Im a strong reader, which has helped me absorb information quickly and apply it in different situations.

What could I improve on?

In Literacy I could have improved on public speaking and discussing texts verbally. I find myself confident in writing, but challenging to articulate my ideas out loud in front of an audience. I dont believe I got any better at giving presentations, but it has given me more opportunities to learn why I am unable to give good presentations.
